# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This README is the central entry point for OctoAcme project management documentation. It gives new team members a quick summary of how projects move from idea to delivery and links to the detailed process guides used across the lifecycle.

## Project Management Process Summary

- **Initiation** — Confirm the business need, define measurable success criteria, identify stakeholders, outline milestones, and make a go/no-go decision before deeper planning begins.
- **Planning** — Turn the approved initiative into a prioritized backlog with acceptance criteria, estimates, milestones, release planning, and an initial view of risks and dependencies.
- **Execution & Tracking** — Deliver work in small increments using recurring team cadences, project-board tracking, pull request quality gates, testing, demos, and blocker escalation paths.
- **Risk Management & Communication** — Maintain a risk register, assign owners and mitigations, use a single source of truth for status, and provide regular stakeholder updates with clear escalation paths.
- **Release & Deployment** — Verify acceptance criteria, CI, security scans, release notes, rollback plans, and smoke tests before staged deployment, post-release validation, and stakeholder communication.
- **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement** — Review what went well, what should improve, and which actions to track after sprints, releases, milestones, and incidents so the team can keep iterating on the process.
